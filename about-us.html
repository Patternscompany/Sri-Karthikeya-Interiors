About Us

Welcome To Sri Kartikeya


Sri Kartikeya Constructions and Interiors, based in Hyderabad, is one of the most experienced and respected builders in the industry, specializing in building and civil construction across diverse market segments. We excel in transforming our clients' ideas and visions into award-winning projects, consistently exceeding expectations for safety, quality, and functionality. Our commitment to delivering finished products that stand the test of time is demonstrated through our wide range of construction solutions and high-quality work. We offer comprehensive services for all your home needs, including Turn-Key Contracting, Project Management Contracting, design-build, and self-performing Interior Design services. Our business thrives on client satisfaction, achieved by maintaining quality throughout every stage of the construction process—from budget advice, planning, and procurement to design, onsite construction, and handover.


We are committed to provide the highest level of professionalism, service response, and quality workmanship. We’re dedicated to winning others over with hard work and excellence.

Our vision is pursuing excellence through dedication, experience and disciplined employees with an ongoing passion to deliver quality, timely and profitable projects.
Our values are part of our DNA. Sri Kartikeya core values are Quality, Ethics and Innovation. We have created a vibrant company culture where ideas can blossom and success can flourish.
Outstanding Design & Timely Execution Respect of all client needs. We Provide right team for your project. We build positive, long-term relationships with our customers.
