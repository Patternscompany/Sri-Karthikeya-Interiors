Life at Sri Kartikeya

Sri Kartikeya Constructions and Interiors is a dynamic, fast-growing and a reliable Real Estate company based in Hyderabad. Our employees strongly believe that each of the tasks and actions they perform, must be primarily aimed at adding value to company or services. We offer our employees ample career prospects, with the security of an excellent salary and other benefits. We firmly believe that it is the people who make all the difference; that each team member contributes directly to company's growth and success. We always look for people who aspire to bring in wealth of experience, skills and knowledge to the company. Candidates who are willing to work with us can email your resume.
